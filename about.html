About Annamaria

Annamaria started singing in a choir at the age of seven, in her home town of Debrecen, Hungary. Diligent and passionate, she joined the world-famous Cantemus Girls Choir, then the Pro Musica Girls Choir, which performed in many countries.

Alongside choir practice, Annamaria started to learn the piano, and entered the Franz Liszt Academy. She learned the history and theory of classical music, and became highly trained in piano and organ, under the guidance of exceptional teachers. In 1993, she graduated with a degree in choir conduction.

In Italy, Annamaria pursued her interest in Italian and Latin, in which are sung many operas and liturgic pieces. She went on to become a qualified teacher of the Italian language.

Annamaria moved to the United Kingdom in 2008, and has been teaching music here since 2009. She applied to, and received, the Qualified Teacher Status (QTS) from the National College for Teaching and Leadership.
